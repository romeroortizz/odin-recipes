Chicken Soup Recipe

Home page

This miso chicken noodle soup, with thick, chewy udon noodles and a savory miso broth, is a tasty—and very easy—remake of traditional chicken noodle soup.

This recipe is easy to create and requires only a few ingredients that are less common such as udon noodles, miso paste and bagel seasoning

ingredients:

  • 1 1/1 cups of water
  • 8.5oz frozen udon noodles
  • 4oz rotisserie chicken, shredded
  • 1 tablespoon white miso paste
  • everything bagel seasoning,to taste

Directions

  1. In saucepan over medium high heat, heat water to boiling. Add udon noodles. cook until noodles are softened, 4 to 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken. Turn heat to low. REmove 2 to 3 ts water, and stir miso paste into water until smooth.
  3. ladle soup into bowls and garnish with everything bagel seasoning
